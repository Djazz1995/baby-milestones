Baby Milestones · Design System

Calm, rounded, teal-green. Crisp white cards on a soft canvas, one calm accent, big radii, soft shadows, iOS-native chrome. Companion to design/DESIGN_SYSTEM.md. Fonts: Fraunces (serif, name/age/titles only) + Inter.

Typography

Warm serif (Fraunces) for the emotional anchors: baby name, age indicator, titles. Inter for everything else. Body never below 17 (grandparent-legible).

Elevation

Soft, not flat. Gentle diffuse shadows (low opacity, large blur). Stronger soft shadow on FAB + sheets. Never hard/dark drop shadows.
