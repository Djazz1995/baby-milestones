Multiple babies · Phase 2

A family can have several children. The Timeline header baby chip is a child switcher (chevron = tappable): pick a child and the age indicator + timeline follow it. "All children" is an optional combined feed (each card tagged with the baby). "Add a baby" (owner) routes to the same Create-baby screen as onboarding. Also reachable from Settings → Baby → Add a baby.

Pregnancy: no name yet, gender optional

Expecting parents often have no name (just a bump nickname) and may not know the gender. Name + nickname + gender are all optional in pregnancy; the header reads the week count, the avatar falls back to a sprout, and name becomes required at birth (record-birth). Gender is optional and neutral. "Surprise" is a first-class choice; no gendered colours anywhere.

Growth tracking · measurements on moments

A moment can carry an optional weight + length (the "Add measurements" row in Add-moment). Moments with a measurement show a growth chip, and a Growth chart (Phase 3) is derived from them — no separate growth-entry to keep in sync. Attach the numbers to the memory (the check-up photo) and the curve builds itself.

Privacy & compliance flows

The flows behind Settings that App Store / GDPR require: account deletion (Apple 5.1.1(v), double-confirm + clear consequences), data export (GDPR portability), and a report path on content (UGC 1.2). Delete wording differs by role: sole owner erases the whole archive; a co-owner keeps the shared archive (their content anonymized).
